RULES:

You will be provided with two initial cards either (1 to 10) or any card among (A, J, Q, K). Each card will have its own value that raises the total score to a certain amount. Each of the cards from 1 to 10 have a score of 1 to 10 respectively. The card 'A' has a score of 11 and each of the cards 'J', 'Q' and 'K' will be counted as 10. Getting a total score of 21 will make you hit the Blackjack. You can draw new cards one by one until you hit the Blackjack. If your score exceeded 21 then you're out of the game.

HOW TO PLAY:

Click "START GAME" button to start the game. You will be provided with your two initial cards. You can see your cards and your score at their respective sections. If you didnt have enough score you can draw a new card by clicking on the "NEW CARD" button. You cant draw another card when you hit a blackjack or when youre out of the game. That's all and Good luck!
